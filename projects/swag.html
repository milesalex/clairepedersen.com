Why do companies love giving people garbage?

Dropbox + Odd Pears

Most free stuff given out by tech companies generally isn't great. You don’t want it, you won’t use it, and the only place it really belongs is in the trash. At Dropbox we tried to push the standard for corporate swag and reinvent it. We were tired of seeing t-shirts with the logo just slapped on. So we partnered with Odd Pears to give our employees something they would like and wear more than once.
